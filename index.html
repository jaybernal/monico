about us

Longtime time dog trainer and dog behavior consultant Nathan Monico has received his training from world renown dog behaviorist Cheri Lucas. He has privately worked in the homes of families with dogs who may have aggression, anxiety, obsessive, possessive and even basic obedience. Nathan has intuitive and effective ways of having clients learn the values of leadership and calm assertive energy. He also educates clients on dog psychology and differences between principles and techniques. Nathan also served the community by giving seminars and public appearances to local humane societies and clubs.

He believes he owes a great deal of gratitude towards dogs and is humbled by all that support his mission of hope and balance to the clients that want a stronger relationship with their dog.
